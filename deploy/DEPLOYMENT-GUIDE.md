## 🌐 Recommended: Netlify Deployment (FREE)

### Step 1: Sign Up for Netlify
1. Go to [netlify.com](https://netlify.com)
2. Sign up with your email (free account)
3. Verify your email address

### Step 2: Deploy Your Site
1. **Drag & Drop Method:**
   - Log into Netlify dashboard
   - Drag the entire `deploy` folder to the deployment area
   - Your site goes live instantly at `https://random-name.netlify.app`

2. **Alternative: Manual Upload:**
   - Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
   - Select all files in this deploy folder
   - Upload and deploy

### Step 3: Custom Domain Setup
**Your Domain: `dst.bobbybrockinsurance.com`**

**In Netlify:**
1. Go to Site Settings → Domain Management
2. Click "Add custom domain"
3. Enter: `dst.bobbybrockinsurance.com`
4. Netlify will provide you with a target URL (like `amazing-name-123456.netlify.app`)

**In Your Current Hosting (bobbybrockinsurance.com DNS):**
1. Add a CNAME record:
   - **Name:** `dst`
   - **Target:** `[your-netlify-url].netlify.app` (from step 4 above)
2. Save DNS changes (may take 1-24 hours to propagate)

### Step 4: SSL Certificate
- Netlify automatically provides free SSL (HTTPS)
- Your site will be secure with green padlock 🔒
